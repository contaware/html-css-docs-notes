Embed Google Maps

  1. Go to Google Maps.
  2. Search your place.
  3. Press the Share button and choose Embed a map.
  4. Click COPY HTML and paste into your html file.
  5. Look at the grabbed iframe link, in two places there is en, replace it with de, it or fr for other languages.

View Larger Map
