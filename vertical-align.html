The reason vertical-align: middle isn't doing what is desired is because most people do not understand what it's supposed to do.

vertical-align for inline elements

Inline elements can be vertically aligned with respect to the text line they are in (not the whole parent container height). vertical-align: middle must be set for the inline element, unlike for tables where it is specified for the parent:
