The reason vertical-align isn't doing what is desired is because most people do not understand what it's supposed to do. Probably you want to check flexbox to easily align elements vertically.

vertical-align for inline elements

Inline elements can be vertically aligned with respect to the text line they are in (not the whole parent container height). vertical-align must be set for the inline element, unlike for tables where it is specified for the parent:

BIG (watch the alignment when the line wraps) Middle Bottom Top

vertical-align in tables

When used in table cells, vertical-align does what most people expect it to do, it vertically aligns its content:
