Percentage units

The % values are calculated based on the size of the grid container. The items will overflow the container, if a gap is set:

40%
10%
20%
30%

Fractional units

The fr values are more comfortable because they will get the space that's left after the px, % values and the gaps have taken their space. This available space is proportionally subdivided between the fr values:

200px
10%
2fr
1fr
