Flexbox flex

Item 2 has flex-grow: 1 and flex-shrink: 0. The default for flex-grow is 0 (no grow), positive values mean grow in the available space with that proportion. The default for flex-shrink is 1 (shrink), positive values mean shrink to fit with that proportion.
